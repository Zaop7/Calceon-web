Gameplay

Progress through levels

Go on an adventure in the mysterious dungeons of Calceon~

Travel through 20 levels and 4 different biomes on your journey for knowledge!

Face enemies

Those who obscure your path must be swiftly defeated!

Solve problems

Train your skills on a variety of mathematical and logical exercises!

The game offers an option for training, where you can practice even more.

The game is designed for both middle and high school students, but it will entertain anyone who loves smart challenges. With both a story and a practice mode, you can choose whether to train your skills or experience an adventure.
