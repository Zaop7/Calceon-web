Discover a world where every problem is the key to the next adventure.

Our math game combines fun gameplay mechanics with solving logical and mathematical problems. Progress through levels, collect books with problems, face enemies, and move forward only when your mind prevails.

The game is designed for both middle and high school students, but it will entertain anyone who loves smart challenges. With a combination of story and practice modes, you can choose whether to train or experience an adventure.
